# Research Deliverables

Use this reference when the task needs a structured UX research summary, usability assessment, or findings document.

## Recommended Research Summary Shape

```md
# UX Research: [Feature or Surface]

## Research Goal
- What decision or uncertainty this analysis should inform

## Evidence Reviewed
- User feedback
- Support themes
- Analytics notes
- Existing UI or prototype
- Prior research

## User Goals and Context
- What users are trying to accomplish
- Relevant constraints or usage context

## Key Findings
- Finding
- Why it matters
- Confidence: High / Medium / Low

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes
- Issues that may affect clarity, access, or task completion

## Recommendations
- Immediate fixes
- Medium-term improvements
- Follow-up research needed

## Open Questions
- What still needs direct evidence or stakeholder clarification
```

## Usability Review Template

```md
# Usability Review: [Surface]

## What Works
- Strong patterns worth preserving

## Friction Points
- Where users are likely to hesitate, misread, or fail

## Severity
- High / Medium / Low per issue

## Recommendation
- Concrete action to reduce friction
```

## Evidence Ladder

- Direct evidence: observed behavior, provided user quotes, measured outcomes
- Strong inference: repeated pattern strongly implied by multiple artifacts
- Hypothesis: plausible but not yet validated

Always label which level a claim belongs to.

## Recommendation Checklist

- Tie each recommendation to a finding
- Explain likely user impact
- Avoid vague advice without an action
- Separate validated issues from speculative opportunities
- Call out when follow-up research is needed before a major decision
